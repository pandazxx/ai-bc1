# Requirements

## Technical Requirements

- Engine: `Unity`
- Repository: `github`
- CI/CD: `github action`

## Game Requirements

- Copycat of Chrome browser's famous dinosaur game.
- Horizontal scroll game
- Player control a dinosaur. It will keep moving forward with a constant speed. Player can only control it to jump.
- Obstacles will be randomly generated infront of the dinosaur.
- Game over once dinosaur hit any obstacle.
- Camera follows the dinosaur.

## Art Requirements

- Art style: Pixel art
